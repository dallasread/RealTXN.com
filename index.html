Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How long before I can start taking donations?

    As soon as you sign up and input your information. Its that quick. The whole process takes about five minutes.

  2. Is this method of giving secure?

    Security is one of our top priorities. We don’t store any sensitive data (credit card numbers, etc). We hand all of those concerns over to our payment processor, Stripe.

  3. What are the different types of payments you can process?

    All major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also accept Visa/Mastercard debit cards from banks such as TD, CIBC, RBC, and BMO.

  4. How will I know a donation has been made?

    An email confirmation will be sent to both the giver and the church. It will contain a receipt for the giver and a notification for the church.

  5. How soon does the transaction go into the churches account?

    You will be paid on a seven day rolling basis. E.g., you will receive funds for your March 1st charges on March 8th.

  6. What happens if there are insufficient funds?

    Transactions are validated in real time to ensure the funds are available. In the case of a failed recurring payment, the donor will receive a notification to update their card information. We'll try to process the transaction 2 more times over the next 2 days, then deactivate the card.

  7. Can I use Instagive for other transactions than tithes and offering?

    You can do payments for camp registration, conferences, building funds, special offerings or any other causes.

  8. Why do you charge fees on transactions?

    Instead of contracts, setup fees, or monthly plans, our rates are pay-as-you-grow. Instagive’s rate of only 3.9% + 60¢ per transaction is very competitive and allows us to keep offering you the best service possible.

  9. What does the transaction look like in my donor's bank account?

    Your organization's name will be displayed on the bank statement.

  10. How is your system any different than others?

    Our main focus is to help non profits and charities. We also offer a completely customizable app for your organizaiton. Not to mention that our system is already setup for one time and recurring payments.

  11. Do I have to be a charity to use Instagive.io?

    No! At this stage in our company, we've chosen to focus on signing up charities and non-profits, but we're more than happy to welcome companies and for-profits.
